Setup Instructions

  1. Cut out the sticker above along the dashed lines
  2. Apply the sticker directly onto the security panel
  3. Take a photo of the panel with the sticker visible
  4. Upload the photo in the Admin dashboard and place annotation overlays

For Technicians

  1. Scan the QR code on the sticker with your phone camera
  2. Allow camera access when prompted
  3. Point your phone at the barcode marker on the sticker
  4. Color-coded wiring labels will appear overlaid on the panel
